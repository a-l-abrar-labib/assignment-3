Program Sections

We believe in learning through play. Through engaging drills, mini-games, and age-appropriate training sessions, our players not only develop hockey skills but also cultivate a sense of teamwork and sportsmanship.

Junior Program

To provide a taste of real competition, our Junior Program includes fun and friendly mini-tournaments and events. These experiences create lasting memories and friendships, reinforcing the joy of playing hockey.

Teenager Program

The Teenage Program at Blizzard Blitz is tailored for players aged 13 to 17 who are ready to take their hockey journey to the next level. This phase emphasizes refining individual skills, understanding team dynamics, and preparing for more competitive play.

Professional Program

Success at the professional level requires mental resilience. The Professional Program includes specialized mental conditioning sessions to help athletes navigate the pressures of high-stakes competition, maintain focus, and perform at their absolute best.

Hockey Training

Yes, we offer private lessons for individuals of all ages. Whether your child is just starting or looking to refine specific skills, our experienced coaches provide personalized attention to ensure a focused and effective learning experience.
